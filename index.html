About the Church

Shepherd circle ministries is a continuation of Jesus Christ Ministry.....(John 14:12; "Verily, verily, I say unto you, He that believeth on Me, the works that I do shall he do also, and greater works than these shall he do, because I go unto My Father") AIMS AND OBJECTIVES OF SHEPHERD CIRCLE MINISTRIES ARE;

  • 1. TO PROPAGATE THE GOSPEL OF JESUS CHRIST TO THE WORLD.
  • 2. TO REACH OUT TO THE LOST SOULS THROUGH EVANGELISM AND PREACHING OF THE WORLD OF GOD.
  • 3. TO PROMOTE DAILY BIBLE READING, TEACHING AND MEDITATION....
